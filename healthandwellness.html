MASSAGE

Our massage therapists personalize each session for each customer, kneading muscles to ease tension, stress, and enhance circulation.

Types of massage

Relaxing Massage
With the purpose of releasing physical and mental strain,
the massage employs gradual and strong techniques.
Swedish Massage
With soft and wide strokes, the massage employs a range of soothing techniques.
The benefits of this massage include improved circulation and musculoskeletal detoxification.
Deep Tissue
Pressure and movement are used to release deep layers of tissue, such as tendons,
ligaments, and fascia, in this strong, manipulative therapy (the protective layer surrounding muscles, bones, and joints).
